# movie-industry-insights-report-
This dashboard presents an interactive analysis of the global movie industry, focusing on trends in movie releases across time, languages, genres, and production companies. It provides a clear overview of total movies released, release patterns by decade and season, and growth trends over selected time periods.

The visuals highlight the distribution of movies by language, identify the top production companies by number of releases, and showcase the most popular genres based on movie count. Time-based analysis reveals how movie production has evolved from earlier decades to recent years, reflecting shifts in audience demand and industry expansion.

Interactive filters for year range, genre, and language enable users to explore specific segments of the movie industry, compare trends, and gain actionable insights. Overall, the dashboard supports data-driven understanding of production patterns, content diversity, and long-term growth within the film industry.
